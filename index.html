How it works

Four steps.
One income stream.

01
Fund a hostel
You provide capital to deploy solar-powered WiFi infrastructure into one or more student hostels. No hardware management, no operations.
02
We deploy and operate
FroNet installs the network, manages access points, handles student authentication, support, and all ongoing operations. Fully.
03
Students subscribe
Students pay for daily, weekly, or monthly plans via Paystack. Every successful payment flows into your income ledger in real time.
04
You request your earnings
Your 40% accumulates in your dashboard. Request a payout to your bank account whenever you want — full audit trail, always.
